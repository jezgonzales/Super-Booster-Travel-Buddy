Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start taking these supplements before my trip?

For optimal results, start taking Super-Booster Travel Buddy 5-7 days before your journey. This allows your body to build up the beneficial compounds and adapt to time zone changes more effectively. Continue throughout your trip and 2-3 days after returning for complete travel recovery support.

Are these supplements TSA-approved for carry-on luggage?

Yes! Our travel packets are TSA-compliant and designed specifically for carry-on luggage. Each supplement comes in clearly labeled, sealed packets that meet all aviation security requirements. We recommend keeping them in their original packaging with your personal items for easy security screening.

How quickly do the supplements take effect?

The Morning Vitality Blend typically shows effects within 30-45 minutes, providing energy and focus for your day. The Evening Restoration Blend begins working within 20-30 minutes, helping you relax and adjust to new time zones. Full adaptogenic benefits are experienced after 3-5 days of consistent use.

How should I store these supplements while traveling?

Our supplements are specially packaged to maintain potency in various travel conditions. Keep them at room temperature (below 77°F/25°C), away from direct sunlight and moisture. The individual packets are temperature-stable and designed to withstand typical cabin pressure changes during flights.

How are the travel packs packaged?

Each travel kit includes individually sealed, clearly labeled morning and evening packets. The packets are waterproof, tear-resistant, and sized for easy packing. A standard 7-day travel pack contains 7 morning and 7 evening doses, with options for longer trips available.

What's your shipping policy for travel deadlines?

We offer expedited 2-day and overnight shipping options to ensure you receive your supplements before your trip. Orders placed before 2 PM are shipped same-day. For international destinations, we provide DHL Express shipping with delivery typically within 3-5 business days.
